Riste Delov is a dedicated student and aspiring technologist currently pursuing his undergraduate degree in Information Technologies and Computer Engineering (PIT) at the Faculty of Computer Science and Engineering (FINKI) in Skopje, Macedonia. His journey into the world of technology began with a natural curiosity about computers and a passion for problem-solving.

At FINKI Riste has immersed himself in a rigorous academic curriculum that includes programming, algorithms, and software engineering. Beyond the classroom, he actively engages in coding competitions, hackathons, and workshops, where he collaborates with peers and industry professionals to innovate and tackle complex challenges.

Riste's enthusiasm for technology extends beyond academics. He is particularly interested in exploring emerging technologies and their practical applications, such as software development, data analytics, and cybersecurity. His experiences have not only deepened his technical expertise but also honed his teamwork and leadership skills.
